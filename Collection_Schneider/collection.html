It all starts with a dream, a passion, a hobby. You may like painting, like this girl, or even singing, maybe dancing. What your passion is, is not important. What matters is how you choose to pursue it. Are you talented enough? Or do you choose to paint for fun and that's it?

You may think you have an eye for art, but when you think about it there are millions of others with that same eye. Millions of others that have the same skill as you, it's almost impossible to stand out the way you might want too.

Let's look at some famous works, see if you can live up to the standards already put in place by others. There are hundreds and thousands of works better then yours, and you still want to continue?

Look at you, you're a mess, a starving artist. You are always sad, and have always been as such, that will not change whether you choose to pursue art or stop. The misery will keep growing and growing and growing until you break down inside. It will seep into your work, letting everyone know how pathetic you are.

Destroy your creation, let it out, let out the built up resentment you've felt all these years towards your craft. Slash your canvas like your cutting every great artist that's ever come before you. Cut them down, knock them down a peg, show them that you are just as worthy as they are.

Was it all for nothing? You've destroyed your work out of rage, out of anger, and look where that got you. I can tell you're distraught, but don't fear, with your anger you've created a piece even more raw and beautiful. Much like the human soul
